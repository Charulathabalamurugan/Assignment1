About Giva

GIVA was founded in 2019 by Ishendra Agarwal, Nikita Prasad, and Sachin Shetty.
Today, it is one of the fastest-growing D2C brands in India.
Our vision is to “Be the best in Fine Jewellery”.


When we started four years ago, our mission was to be the best option for silver jewellery.
We have since pivoted towards creating accessible luxury.
Apart from silver, we have also recently added gold to our ever expanding catalogue.


Currently, we have 40+ retail stores in 7+ cities across India and are expanding rapidly.
